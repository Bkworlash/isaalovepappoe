OPEN MY EYES

Many use different lenses in reading the Bible, one from a judgmental view, one from a historic view, the other from the view of Heaven and Hell, another from the lens of an angry God and even others who read their own entrenched beliefs in its pages for their selfish and manipulative purposes. But until we view the Bible through the lens of Jesus Christ, His death, burial, resurrection, ascension and seating, we would dabble in confusion, fight one another and even protect our own man-made doctrine to put the children of God in bondage.
This synergistic book unveils the radical message of the finished work of Christ from the cross to the throne. Presented within purely Christo-centric paradigms, this theologically challenging book, “Open My Eyes”, imparts the reader with insightful and spiritually tantalizing ways of conceptualizing and understanding the gospel, especially, when viewed using the lens of Grace, which the Author argues, is actually the Person of a man - Jesus Christ.
The book clearly amplifies Christ above crisis, faith above fear, grace above guilt, righteousness about right-living, justification above condemnation, the Son of God above the sins of men, and heaven above hell. Any reader who attempts to go through the pages of this book will be bequeathed with abundance of Grace as he/she is carefully navigated from their low terrestrial paradigms of sin consciousness imposed upon them by religion, to the high celestial realm of life, which is a product of the grace of the Kingdom of heaven

GRACE SYRUP

This syrup is very much needed to fight against law inflammatory infections like guilt, condemnation, distance, depression, fear and insecurity of salvation. By reading this book, you will be assured by God’s love and built up in Grace.

CHRIST- THE MYSTERY BEHIND THE BIBLE NAMES

Throughout the ages, there is one thing the church has lost sight of, and that is the salvific therapeutic revelation of Jesus Christ in all things the Scriptures talk about. The eyes of the church will be greatly enlightened, illuminated and flooded with light when the enormity of this great aletheia (truth) is systematically, rhythmically and spasmodically dispensed to the church.
Then we will see that Jesus Christ is the centre, the lens, the protagonist and antagonist, the beginning, the revolution, the substance, the body and everything creation reveals. The whole Bible is an admiration, an adumbration, a prefiguration and a harbinger of Christ. In Him, all things consist, co-exist, exist, subsist, persist, inhibits, submits and co-inhere. He's God's transcendent, eminent, prominent, provident Savior and image, the effulgence of His (God's) being, the crescendo of God's voice, the sum total and the consummation of all things. By reading this book, you will come to the discovery that all the Bible names and stories point to Jesus Christ and His plan of dying for the salvation of the world, especially those who believe.
